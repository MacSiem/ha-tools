### 1. CARD VS CONTAINER

- The main wrapper uses `.panel-root` (line ~230), not `.card` or `.container`.
- Inside, `.panel-header`, `.panel-body`, `.tabs`, `.stat-card`, `.tip-card`, `.chart-container` etc. are used.
- No `.card` or `.container` class wrappers found.
- However, `.stat-card` and `.tip-card` are styled as cards with `background: var(--bento-card)` and border-radius.
- Bento design system expects `.card` as a standard card wrapper; `.container` is discouraged.
- **Count:**
  - `.card` wrappers: 0 (no `.card` class)
  - `.container` wrappers: 0
  - Uses `.stat-card` and `.tip-card` as card-like elements instead.
- **Verdict:** No `.card` or `.container` classes used, but `.stat-card` and `.tip-card` serve as cards. This is consistent within the component but diverges from Bento naming conventions.
