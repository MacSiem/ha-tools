8. **ACCESSIBILITY**  
- Color contrast:  
  - Bento tokens use standard HA theme colors, which generally have good contrast.  
  - Hardcoded colors for stat values (#F59E0B, #3B82F6, #10B981) have good contrast on white background.  
  - Inline styles for email HTML colors are standard and readable.  
- Aria labels:  
  - Buttons have no explicit `aria-label` except for price save/cancel buttons (line ~860) which have `aria-label="Save"` and `aria-label="Cancel"`.  
  - Other buttons rely on visible text or emoji icons, which may be insufficient for screen readers.  
  - Tabs buttons have no `role="tab"` or `aria-selected` attributes; no keyboard navigation support visible.  
  - Inputs have associated labels in config editor and schedule tab, good.  
- Missing:  
  - No explicit aria roles or labels on tabs for accessibility.  
  - Buttons with only emoji icons (e.g. tab buttons) may be unclear for screen readers.  
- Verdict: Minor accessibility issues due to missing ARIA roles/labels on tabs and some buttons; color contrast is acceptable.
